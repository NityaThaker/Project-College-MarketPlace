About Us


Welcome to College MarketPlace, your one-stop platform for buying and selling used items within your college community! Our mission is to provide a **safe, affordable, and convenient** marketplace exclusively for students.



Why Did I Built This?


I noticed that many students struggle to find affordable books, gadgets, and essentials. At the same time, students often have items they no longer need but find it hard to sell. This platform solves both problems by connecting students to buy and sell easily!



What College MarketPlace Offer


      Buy & sell used books, gadgets, and furniture.
      Negotiate prices directly with other students.
      College email verification for secure transactions.
      Easy-to-use platform with fast listings.
