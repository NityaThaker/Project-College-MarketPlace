How It Works?


Step 1: Sign Up

To ensure that only students from your college can access the marketplace, you need to sign up using your college email ID. A verification link will be sent to your email. Once verified, you can start using the platform.




Step 2: Browse Listings

Once registered, you can explore various listings posted by students. Items are categorized for easy navigation, and you can use the search bar to find what you need.




Step 3: Negotiate & Buy

Found something you like? Contact the seller through the chat feature or their provided details. You can discuss pricing, item condition, and finalize the deal.




Step 4: Get Receipt

After completing the purchase, you will receive a digital receipt via email. This receipt serves as proof of purchase and includes transaction details.




Step 5: Meet & Exchange

Decide on a safe location within the college campus to meet the seller. Inspect the item before making the payment and ensure everything is as expected.
